Day Pass

A Day Pass offers unlimited rides on the Pune Metro network for an entire day. It is perfect for tourists and passengers who need to travel multiple times in one day.

Features:
  • Unlimited travel for 1 day on all Metro lines.
  • Valid only on the day of purchase.
  • Non-transferable and cannot be refunded.
  • Available at ticket counters and vending machines.
How to Use:
  1. Purchase the Day Pass at any Pune Metro station.
  2. Tap the card/QR at the entry gate for unlimited rides.
  3. Use it at exit gates for each journey until the day ends.
