QR Tickets

QR Tickets can be booked online through the Pune Metro mobile app or website. Simply scan the QR code at the gates to travel without needing a physical ticket.

Features:
  • Book anytime, anywhere using the Pune Metro app.
  • Contactless travel with mobile QR code scanning.
  • Valid only for the booked journey and time.
  • No need to stand in queues at stations.
How to Use:
  1. Download the Pune Metro app and register/login.
  2. Book your journey and generate a QR ticket.
  3. At the entry gate, scan the QR from your phone screen.
  4. Scan again at the exit gate to complete your journey.
