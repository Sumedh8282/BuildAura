Monthly Pass

The Monthly Pass is designed for daily commuters who use Pune Metro regularly. It allows unlimited travel for 30 days, making it cost-effective and convenient.

Features:
  • Unlimited rides for 30 consecutive days.
  • Valid across all Pune Metro routes.
  • Non-transferable and cannot be refunded once issued.
  • Issued at Metro stations (with ID proof if required).
How to Use:
  1. Purchase a Monthly Pass from the counter.
  2. Activate it on the first day of travel.
  3. Tap it at entry and exit gates like a normal ticket.
  4. Valid for 30 days from the activation date.
