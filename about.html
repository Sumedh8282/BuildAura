About Pune Metro

Pune Metro is a fast, safe, and eco-friendly urban transport system designed to make travel in Pune more comfortable and efficient. It aims to reduce traffic congestion and air pollution while providing modern facilities to commuters.

Our Mission

To deliver a world-class metro service that is affordable, reliable, and sustainable for the people of Pune.

Our Vision

To make Pune a smart and green city by providing an eco-friendly transport system that reduces dependency on private vehicles.

Core Values

Safety, Punctuality, Innovation, and Sustainability are the pillars on which Pune Metro is built.
