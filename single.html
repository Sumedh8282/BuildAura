Single Journey Ticket

A Single Journey Ticket allows you to travel one-way between any two stations on the Pune Metro network. It is ideal for passengers who do not travel frequently.

Features:
  • Valid for a single trip between selected stations.
  • Non-transferable and cannot be reused.
  • Must be used on the same day of purchase.
  • Available at ticket counters and ticket vending machines.
How to Use:
  1. Buy the ticket from a Metro station counter or vending machine.
  2. Tap it at the entry gate to start your journey.
  3. Tap it again at the exit gate to complete your journey.
