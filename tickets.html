Pune Metro Ticket Booking

Types of Tickets

Single Journey

One-way travel between two stations.

Return Journey

Travel to your destination and return with one ticket.

Day Pass

Unlimited rides for one day.

Monthly Pass

Unlimited travel for 30 days – best for daily commuters.

QR Tickets

Book via Pune Metro App and scan at the gate.
